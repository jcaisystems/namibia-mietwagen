# About Us

"Sun and Sand Car Rental Windhoek was borne out of our love and passion for road trips in Namibia, and we believe everyone should have the opportunity to experience it too."

## About Us

We were both born in South Africa, however, after living here for many years, we call Namibia our home. To maintain balance and rejuvenate our soul we spend as much time as possible in nature, either bird watching, observing, and photographing wildlife in our national parks, or just appreciating the diverse and vast landscapes our road trips take us through. Oh, and not to forget, being in awe of the star-filled night skies during new moon.

Sun and Sand Car Rental was borne out of our love and passion for road trips in Namibia, and we believe everyone should have the opportunity to experience it too.

The adventure starts with planning the route, pouring over a map, finding areas not visited before or going back to places we love. Over the years, we have learnt that planning means we are prepared and thus frees us up to experience more on our trips. Being prepared, means packing only what you need for the journey, having binoculars, camera, beverages, and snacks accessible, knowing the route, and what can be stocked up on along the way. This makes for easy packing and more time to relax. We recommend staying in one place for 2 or even 3 nights, allowing time to immerse oneself in the area, to slow down and savour the stunning sunrises and sunsets.

Lodge hopping, self-catering, and Airbnb (in Windhoek and Swakopmund only), is a convenient way to experience Namibia without compromising on the need to escape the crowds as many establishments are in remote areas. Lodges and tented camps are designed with space between chalets, providing privacy and a sense of solitude. It is reassuring that the price spectrum is broad, suiting a range of budgets. On a single trip you could consider alternating between full board and self-catering or camping so that you can braai (barbeque/BBQ) and spend the evening around a fire.

We look forward to welcoming you to Namibia where you can also connect with nature, experience the benefits of a digital detox if you chose to do so, and return home feeling rejuvenated and relaxed with memories that will last a lifetime, and possibly draw you back for more.

## FAQ

Do you provide airport transfers:

Transfers are included for 1 or 2 people for rentals of 15 days. For rentals of 5-14 days we will gladly assist with arrangements for a meet & greet, which will be for your costs and added to your quote.

How do I make a reservation and how is the booking confirmed?

Complete your booking or request a quote online. Or you can email us directly at info@sun-sandcarrental.com.Your booking will be confirmed via email once 25% deposit has been paid.

Is there is a minimum rental period and are there conditions for shorter rental periods?

Yes, the minimum rental period is 5 consecutive days. At our discretion, an excess km fee will apply for rentals of 5-7 days if 300km per day is exceeded during the rental period. You will be informed if this applies when you provide us with your planned route.

Do I need a credit card to rent a vehicle?

Either a credit or debit card will be required. The card must be with you when you collect the vehicle as a pre-authorization is required for the security deposit. As this is a non-financial transaction, there should be no bank charges on a credit card if the full amount is released upon return of the vehicle, as per T&C's.

If you do not have a credit card, the security deposit can be paid with a debit card, however a 2% surcharge will apply to cover our bank charge. The security deposit will be refunded when the vehicle is returned in good order. You will be refunded, less the surcharge, after inspection and as per the T&C's. Note, you will also be charged bank fees by your bank for these transactions.

Are Virtual Cards (e.g. Apple Pay) American Express and Diners Club accepted?

We only accept Visa and Mastercard.

Virtual Cards can be used however we will require the original bank card's actual number as the virtual transaction creates a unique number for each transaction thus the pre-authorisation cannot be released on a POS terminal.

Do I need to pay a deposit?

Yes, a 25% deposit is required to secure your booking. Full payment is due 30 days before rental period.

Payment can be paid via an electronic bank transfer into our account or online following the link in the invoice sent to you.

Is insurance available and is personal liability cover included?

SSCR vehicles are insured with a standard insurance including third party cover. The excess on the vehicle, as stipulated in the Car Rental Agreement, will be held as a pre-authorisation on your credit card, so please ensure that your credit card has sufficient funds to allow for this. Should the vehicle be damaged, the damage will be assessed, and the charges deducted from the security deposit. If a reduced liability waiver is purchased, the liability will be limited to the amount stipulated on the terms and conditions, unless in breach of SSCR terms and conditions as stipulated in the terms and conditions. In this instance you will be liable for the full costs of the damage or total loss.

It should be noted that this is NOT a personal liability cover. SSCR highly recommends that travel and personal insurance is taken out prior to your departure to Namibia.

Refer to our Terms and Conditions, clause 6.

What is your cancellation policy:

*   Cancellation occurs more than 30 days before rental: Full refund LESS bank fees
*   Cancellation fee where cancellation occurs 14 to 30 days before rental: 15% of full value of booking
*   Cancellation fee where cancellation occurs 7 to 13 days before rental: 25% of full value of booking
*   Cancellation fee where cancellation occurs 6 to 1 day before rental: 50% of full value of booking
*   Cancellation less than 24 hours before rental or a no-show: 100% of full value of booking

Note: all charges and fees are for the Renters account.

What is the minimum and maximum driver age?

Our minimum driver age is 23 years, and he/she must be in possession of a driver's license for minimum 1 year. The vehicle may not be driven by persons over the age of 85 years.

What documents do I need with me when I pick up the vehicle?

You will need your driver's license and the license of any additional drivers, which must all be valid and unendorsed. If the language on the license is not in English, you will require an International Drivers' license. You will also need a copy of your passport.

Where do I pick up the vehicles and is it possible outside of office hours?

Vehicles are picked up and returned in Windhoek. Our office hours are Monday to Friday 8h00 – 16h30.

With prior arrangement pick up and return outside of office hours can be scheduled, however, is restricted to between 7h00 and 17h30 Monday to Friday. A surcharge will apply for weekends and public holidays and is restricted to between 08H00 until 13H00.

Exceptions can be made; however, this needs to be arranged when making your booking and thus prior to your arrival in Namibia.

What happens if I am delayed for pick up or return?
